Cleaner Turbo — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-04

Cleaner Turbo (the “app”) is developed by Ivan Beschetnikov. We respect your privacy, and this policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

1. On-device processing

The app analyzes your photo and video library locally on your device. Cleaner Turbo does NOT upload your photos, videos, or their contents to any server. Files are only ever removed with your explicit confirmation through the standard iOS deletion dialog.

2. Analytics and stability

We use Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics and crash reports so we can improve the app. This data does not personally identify you and is not used to track you across apps or websites.

3. Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

4. Photo access

The Photos permission is used solely to find duplicates, similar photos, and the other cleanup categories. Access is used only for the app’s on-device functionality.

5. Storage

Your settings and cleaning statistics are stored locally on your device.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the current version is always available at this address.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: php701@gmail.com
